2017 Fellows Workweek

Skill Share Sessions

The short guidebook provides a set of resources co-created by the 2017 Mozilla Fellows.

Workshop Outline

Plans + Scheduling

Focusing on setting goals, planning for the fellowship, and building community capacity.

Danielle - Project planning and measuring impact - 90 min

Berhan - Participatory Action Research - 60 minutes

Closing - Discussion + Share-out - 5 min

Time-Boxing + Transparency

Focusing on scheduling, time-series event planning, appropriate navigation of privacy/transparency.

Kirstie - Building a research group + Time Management - 90 min

Teon - March for Science Brainstorm (gaining feedback) - 60 minutes

Jen - Privacy + Transparency: where to draw the line? (Cop Face Database) - 90 minutes

Closing - Discussion + Share-out - 5 min

Coding + Sustainability

Focusing on building programming skill, reproducible workflows, and protection agains targeted attacks.

Eireann - Malware Information Sharing Platform - 90 min

Kirstie - STEMM Role Models: an intro + invitation to hack - 90 minutes

Zannah - Working Open Workshop Intro - 120 min

Etienne - Targeted Attacks - 60 minutes

Bruno - Reproducible Papers: Code with Nix, Packrat, KnitR, and LaTeX - 60 minutes

Teon - Basic Python Programming Skills - 90 minutes

Bruno - Bioinformatics w/ Bionode.js - 90 minutes

Closing - Discussion + Share-out - 5 min

Security + Support

Focusing on digital security tips, reverse engineering and QA.

Eireann - Reverse Engineering a Pacemaker Programmer - 120 min

Etienne + Berhan - Digital Security: be safe but communicate - 120 minutes

Closing - Discussion + Share-out - 5 min
